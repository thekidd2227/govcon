# GovCon Customer Avatars

> Stub — to be populated with buyer personas for ARCG Systems GovCon marketing.

## Planned Avatars

- **Federal Contracting Officer** — micro-purchase and simplified acquisition buyers
- **State/Local Procurement Lead** — MBE/DBE/SBE set-aside buyers
- **Prime Contractor PM** — subcontracting compliance and capacity gap fills
- **Facilities Director** — pest control, janitorial, and facility support decision-makers

## Fields Per Avatar

- Role / title
- Agency type (federal, state, local, prime)
- Primary pain points
- Buying authority and threshold
- Preferred communication channel
- Content that resonates
- Objections and trust signals needed
