## Long-Form Positioning

ARCG Systems (Ariel's River Contracting Group LLC) is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) execution partner for federal, state, and local agencies and the primes who serve them. ARCG is structured for the work that gets done under $250K — micro-purchases, simplified acquisitions, set-aside actions, and prime subcontracts — where the buyer needs a vendor who is documented, certified, and reachable.

ARCG operates five service lanes under one vendor identity: pest control & extermination (primary NAICS 561710), translation & interpretation, staffing & operational support, janitorial & facility support, and emergency & relief services. ARCG runs a prime-managed, subcontractor-supported delivery model — ARCG holds the contract and the documentation, and qualified subcontractors execute scope where licensing, bonding, or geography requires it.

ARCG's promise to buyers is procurement-aware behavior: a clean capability statement, an active SAM.gov record, current certifications, a 24–72 hour response window, and a single accountable POC.

## Certifications — How to Use Them Without Overclaiming

- **SDVOSB:** Reference SBA VetCert status. Use the phrase "SDVOSB-certified through SBA VetCert" where appropriate. Do not say "veteran-owned" without "SDVOSB-certified" when the audience is procurement.
- **MBE / SBE / DBE:** Always cite the issuing jurisdiction (state agency, certifying authority). Don't blend state certifications with federal ones.
- **HUBZone:** Only claim HUBZone in jurisdictions and timeframes where the certification is verifiably active. Never imply HUBZone where it isn't current.
- **No federal awards claims** unless the award is documented and the buyer has been notified.
- **No "approved vendor" or "preferred vendor" language** for agencies where that status is not in writing.

## Competitor Contrast

- **vs. large facility primes (ABM, ISS, Aramark scale):** ARCG is the responsive, certified, under-$250K alternative. The primes are built for $5M+ contracts. ARCG is built for the work that gets purchased without a multi-volume RFP.
- **vs. single-lane local vendors:** ARCG is the multi-lane vendor with documented certifications and a single POC. Buyers don't have to chase five vendors.
- **vs. "self-certified veteran-owned" vendors:** ARCG holds SDVOSB through SBA VetCert. The certification is verifiable.
- **vs. shell socioeconomic primes:** ARCG self-performs a defined scope and only subs where licensing, bonding, or geography genuinely requires it.

## No-Go Claims / Blocked Claims

Never use the following — across content, capability statement, website, LinkedIn, email, decks:

- "Guaranteed award" / "guaranteed savings" / "guaranteed ROI" / "we guarantee"
- "Award-winning" (unless a specific named award is documented)
- "Trusted by federal agencies" (unless a documented federal client exists and consent is granted)
- "Current federal client" (unless documented and consent is granted)
- "Proven federal results"
- "HIPAA certified"
- "SOC 2 certified"
- "FDA approved"
- "Cleared" / "clearance-ready" unless an active cleared workforce is documented
- "Bonded for $X" unless verified bonding is in place
- "Licensed in all 50 states" unless verifiably true per lane
- "Preferred vendor of [agency]" unless contract or letter is in hand
- Named-client testimonials without written consent
