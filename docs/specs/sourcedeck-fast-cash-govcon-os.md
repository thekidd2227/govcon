# SourceDeck Fast-Cash GovCon OS Spec

## Product goal

Help Charlie find, score, source, quote, and track federal opportunities under $250K with legal outreach controls and subcontractor-supported execution.

## Required modules

- fast-cash-radar
- outreach-window
- charlie-profile
- micro-quote-packet
- sub-source-now
- daily-fast-cash-brief
- cor-safe-crm
- govcon-record-export

## Decisions

- QUOTE_NOW
- SOURCE_SUB_FIRST
- SAFE_OUTREACH_FIRST
- WATCH
- KILL
- MORE_RESEARCH_NEEDED

## Required outputs

Each opportunity must show:

- dollar lane
- fast-cash score
- quote readiness
- sub/vendor readiness
- outreach status
- legal communication guidance
- missing facts
- margin stress
- immediate next action
