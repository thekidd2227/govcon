# Legal Outreach Window Doctrine

## Purpose

Help ARCG build federal familiarity without creating procurement-integrity, unfair-advantage, or restricted-communication risk.

## Status labels

- GREEN_GENERAL_CAPABILITY_INTRO
- GREEN_PRE_SOLICITATION
- GREEN_POST_AWARD
- YELLOW_PUBLIC_QA_ONLY
- RED_RESTRICTED
- UNKNOWN_RESEARCH_FIRST

## Safe outreach

Safe outreach is capability-oriented and not tied to influencing an active solicitation.

Examples:

- introduce ARCG capability
- ask for correct vendor-day or small-business-office process
- respond to RFI/Sources Sought through official channel
- request public market-research participation route
- follow up after public industry day

## Blocked outreach

Do not ask for:

- source-selection information
- incumbent pricing
- nonpublic evaluation preferences
- inside guidance
- tailored advice for an active solicitation
- side-channel COR/program help during an active procurement

## Rule

When an active solicitation is released, use only the listed POC, official Q&A, amendment process, or solicitation instructions.
